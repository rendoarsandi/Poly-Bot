# Product Guide: PolyArb-15m

## 1. Overview
PolyArb-15m is a high-frequency trading bot designed to automate volatility arbitrage on Polymarket's 15-minute crypto binary option markets. The bot acts as a Market Maker, leveraging the "Gabagool" strategy to place limit orders on both "Yes" and "No" sides of a market, ensuring a combined entry price that guarantees profitability (Total Cost < $1.00 payout).

## 2. Goals
-   **Automate Arbitrage:** Continuously scan and execute trades where the "Discount Sum" (Cost of Yes + Cost of No) is mathematically profitable (target $0.95 - $0.98).
-   **Market Making:** Provide liquidity by placing limit orders rather than taking market prices.
-   **Risk Management:** Maintain delta neutrality through "Inventory Skew Management" and automated risk caps.
-   **Capital Efficiency:** Automatically redeem winning shares to recycle capital for the next trading window.

## 3. User Experience
-   **Interface:** Headless Command Line Interface (CLI).
-   **Interaction:** The user launches the bot via a terminal command (e.g., `go run cmd/bot/main.go`).
-   **Feedback:** Real-time operation status, trade executions, and error alerts are streamed to the console logs. No graphical interface or interactive TUI is required.

## 4. Strategic Logic (The "Gabagool" Algorithm)
-   **Discount Sum:** Ensures entry cost for a pair of shares is below the $1.00 payout.
-   **Ladder Quoting:** Places multiple buy orders at graduated price levels to capture volatility wicks.
-   **Rebalancing:** Aggressively adjusts bids or sells inventory if one side fills disproportionately to prevent exposure (Delta Neutrality).

## 5. Key Features
-   **Market Scanner:** Auto-detects active crypto markets ending in 15-30 minutes.
-   **Sum-Check Engine:** Validates profitability (Target_Bid_Yes + Target_Bid_No + Fees < 1.00) before ordering.
-   **Auto-Redemption:** Claims payouts immediately upon market resolution.
-   **Exposure Limit:** Hard kill switch if open positions or unmatched inventory exceed defined safety thresholds.
